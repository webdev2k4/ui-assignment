How to Buy on E-Com Shop

Follow these simple steps to make a purchase on our platform.

1. Browse Products

Find products by browsing categories, using the search bar, or exploring brand pages.

You can browse products in several ways:

  • Navigate through our category menus from the top navigation bar
  • Use the search bar at the top of the page to search for specific products
  • Visit brand shops such as Nike, Adidas, etc.
  • Check out Flash Sales on the homepage for limited-time deals

2. Select a Product

Click on a product that interests you to view its details page.

On the product details page, you can:

  • View multiple product images
  • Read detailed product descriptions and specifications
  • Check product ratings and reviews from other customers
  • View available variants (colors, sizes, etc.)
  • See shipping information

3. Add to Cart

Once you've chosen a product, select any options (size, color, quantity) and click "Add to Cart".

Tip: You can continue shopping and add more items to your cart before checkout.

4. Review Your Cart

Click on the cart icon in the top-right corner to view your selected items.

In your cart, you can:

  • Review all selected items
  • Adjust quantities or remove items
  • Apply discount codes or vouchers
  • See the subtotal and estimated shipping costs

5. Proceed to Checkout

When you're ready to complete your purchase, click the "Checkout" button.

During checkout:

  • Enter or select your shipping address
  • Choose your preferred shipping method
  • Select your payment method
  • Review your order details one final time

Note: You'll need to be logged in to complete a purchase. If you don't have an account, you'll be prompted to create one or log in during checkout.

6. Place Your Order

Once everything looks correct, click "Place Order" to complete your purchase.

After placing your order:

  • You'll receive an order confirmation on screen
  • A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address
  • You can track your order status in the "My Orders" section of your account

Need More Help?

If you have any questions or encounter issues during the purchasing process, please:

  • Visit our Help Center for more information
  • Contact our customer service team via the "Contact Us" page
  • Use the live chat feature available on our website
